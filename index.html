About the Artist

Adrian Paolo David is an artist based in Capas, Tarlac. As he began his career in 2010, he aspires to achieve surrealism as his primary art style. His work highlights the beauty of both the Philippines and being a Filipino, drawing inspiration from Philippine cultural heritage. His paintings reflect his life experiences, socio-cultural events, and loved ones. While acrylic on canvas is his preferred medium, he also works on various mediums, including oil, mixed media, and sculptures.
